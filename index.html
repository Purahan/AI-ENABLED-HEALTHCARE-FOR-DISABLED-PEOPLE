Problem

Problem of People with disabilities

People with disabilities have far more health-care needs and are therefore more vulnerable to the impact of low quality or inaccessible health-care services than others. Couple this with poverty, and we have consequences that are far more awful and tragic.

People with disabilities may be at increased risk of developing chronic conditions.

We tend to forget that it can get unavoidable for these people to neglect their health.

Solution

AI Enabled Smart Watch and connected Mobile App

Fortunately, we have a few devices like health bands or smart watches which can read a couple of health parameters like heart rate, blood pressure or sleep patterns of a person. If those health parameter records are stored at regular rate, we can have an AI application which can alert the patent or the administrator when it starts recognizing an abrupt pattern of values.

This information will be displayed to the family members and connected Hospitals, to raise the alert notifications, so that immediate help can be provided.
